Swiss Championships

The Acro Swiss Championships takes place together with the AWQ in Brienz. The ACBEO organizes the events together with the Swiss Acro League and the Swiss Hägegleiter Association. The Swiss Championship is also part of the Swiss Acro Tour. The SAT will be for the Swiss pilots only and the AWQ is open for every other international pilot who would like to compete. Because Switserland is one of the best places to fly in the alps, you will see a lot of young and strong pilots competing in the SAT competition. At the same time, because the AWQ takes place, we will see a lot of newcomers into the world of acro, as well as some familiar pilots from last year who will try to earn their way into the AWT for next year.

If you are interested to fly competition for the first time as well, make sure to register because the AWQ event is open for every pilot in the world!
