Air et Aventure's Nest Games

Air et Aventure's Nest Games is the first competition of the season and the first competition that will be held over ground. St Hilaire du Touvet is a famous paragliding location because of it's beautiful scenery and easy take-offs for beginner and intermediate pilots. This will be an AWT event, that means that the top 12 best pilots in the world will be competing here. So for all the pilots that will be around, make sure to come and have a look on the 9th of June because all the pilots are highly motivated and you will see some new tricks and combinations that have never been performed before in competition. So stay tuned and hope to see you around!
