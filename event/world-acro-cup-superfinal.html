World Acro Cup Superfinal

The superfinal of the AWT and AWQ of 2023. The moment where we all are waiting for. The final competition of the season where the Acro World Tour Champion will be crowned.

This competition will be like no other. Oludeniz is one of the most famous paragliding and acro training place to fly. The pilots will have more altitude than ever before so they need to be really creative with their runs because of the amount of tricks they can perform in one run and no repititions are allowed. In this competition you will see the best coming out of all the pilots and most importantly, because it is the last competition of the season, there will be a lot of pressure on all the pilots.

It will also be an exciting event for the AWQ pilots because the top 2 of the overall results of the AWQ will be promoted to fly in the AWT in 2024. But at the same time, because 2 pilots are being promoted, the 2 last pilots of the AWT overall ranking will be demoted to the AWQ 2024. So we are super excited what will happen in the superfinal of the season!
