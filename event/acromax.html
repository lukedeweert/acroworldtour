Acromax

Acromax is back! Because of the broken road last year, the competition had to be canceled but we are happy to announce that we will be back in Acromax this year! Acromax is a very familiar acro competition location for many pilots. They've been loyal to the acro community to organise an event for many years already. Also the most recent Acrobatic World Championships in 2021 have been held in this place where Bicho Carrera became World Champion.

The flying conditions in Acromax are always tricky which makes it more interesting for the pro pilots to fly. They will have to face more difficulties which results in the pilots having to show off their overall experience in different types of air.

Acromax will be the first event of the season where both AWT and AWQ pilolts will fly in the same box. It will also be the first event of 2023 where the pilots will have to land on a raft, which will add more difficulties and some specific pilots will have an advantage on the landing. So the results might be really interesting!

At the same time the Italian Championships will take place. All the best pilots from Italy will come to this event to battle against each other where we will see who will be the best pilot in the country!
